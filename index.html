About the Uin Raden Fatah Laboratory

UIN Raden Fatah Palembang has several laboratories that support academic and research activities. The following are several laboratories at UIN Raden Fatah:

  • Environmental Laboratory
  • Software Laboratory
  • Animal Systematics Laboratory
  • General Computer Laboratory A

The UIN Raden Fatah Environmental Laboratory has signed an MoU with UPTD Environmental Laboratory Service Environment and Land of South Sumatra Province. The Software Laboratory organizes activities computer-based lectures and practicums for students. Animal Systematics Laboratory, Faculty of Biology, UGM has received a visit from UIN Raden Fatah Palembang for a discussion on taxidermy and DNA forensics. General Computer Laboratory A is equipped with 36 computers with Intel core i7-7700 processors.

UIN Raden Fatah Palembang also has an International Standard Integrated Laboratory which is used as a venue carry out the Police Academy entrance test.
